Demographics

Population: Approximately 1.2 million

Languages: Haitian Creole, French

Religion: Predominantly Roman Catholic, with Protestant and Vodou elements

Economy

Principal port and commercial center of Haiti

Major products: textiles, cement, soap, food processing

Culture

Notable sites: Marché en Fer, MUPANAH, Gingerbread Houses

Education

Home to several universities, including the University of Haiti (UEH)

Attractions

Champ de Mars, Musée d'Art Haïtien, El-Saieh Gallery

Climate

Tropical wet and dry climate

Average temperatures: 23°C (January) to 28°C (July)

Rainy season: April to October
