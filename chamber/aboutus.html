About Us

The Port-au-Prince Chamber of Commerce is dedicated to fostering a vibrant and sustainable business environment in Port-au-Prince, Haiti. Our mission is to support local businesses, promote economic development, and enhance the quality of life for the community.

Our History

Founded in 1895, the Port-au-Prince Chamber of Commerce has been a cornerstone of the business community for over a centuary. Our organization has grown to include a diverse range of businesses, from small startups to large corporations, all working together to create a thriving economic landscape.

Our Mission

Our mission is to:

  • Advocate for the interests of local businesses at the municipal, regional, and national levels.
  • Provide valuable resources, training, and networking opportunities for our members.
  • Promote Port-au-Prince as a premier destination for business and tourism.
  • Support initiatives that enhance the economic and social well-being of our community.
