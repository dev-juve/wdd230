Port-au-Prince Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The site name selected is "Port-au-Prince Chamber of Commerce". This name was chosen because it accurately represents the city in which the chamber operates and highlights its focus on business and community development within Port-au-Prince.

Site Purpose

The primary purpose of the Port-au-Prince Chamber of Commerce website is to serve as a hub for business information and community events. Specifically, the site aims to:

  • Promote local businesses and provide resources for business development.
  • Offer a platform for community engagement and event information.

Scenarios

Potential questions that the site visitors might ask include:

  • What business networking events are scheduled this month?
  • How can I contact the board of directors of the Chamber?
  • What are the latest economic development initiatives in Port-au-Prince?

Color Schema

The chosen color palette reflects the professional and welcoming tone of the Chamber of Commerce:

#0D1B2A (Headings, Primary Buttons)
#1B263B (Subheadings, Secondary Buttons)
#415A77 (Backgrounds, Footers)
#778DA9 (Accents, Links)
#E0E1DD (Text, Content Backgrounds)

Typography

The primary font chosen for the website is 'Roboto'. This font will be used throughout the site as follows:

  • Headings: Roboto Bold
  • Body Text: Roboto Regular
  • Special Sections: Roboto Italic (for quotes or highlighted information)
